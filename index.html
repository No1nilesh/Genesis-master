GENESIS 2023

Department of Computer Application

"GENESIS" symbolizes the "Rise of the Next Generation." The world keeps advancing when it comes to Technology: Typewriters were replaced by Computers, Telephones by Mobile Phones. "GENESIS" will compromise of various "on-stage" and "off-stage" competitions in the field of IT. This is a learning process by which student will gain an additional knowledge and experience under various aspects in the IT field such as coding, gaming, Photoshop, PC assembling, etc. This will boost the morale and confidence of the students to compete in the current and future IT Field. This will help them to get extra experience in the field of their current study which would act as added study reference to the students. They will get exposure to practical, in a competitive way.
